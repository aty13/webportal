Pinbox Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Pinbox ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Email Address (Optional): If you choose to sign in or contact support, we may collect your email address.
  • Photos: Photos you add to your pins are stored locally on your device by default.

Information Automatically Collected

  • Location Data: We use your location data to display your position on the map and to help you create pins at your current location. This data is processed locally on your device unless you choose to sync it.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage data to help us improve the app.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the App's functionality (displaying maps, saving pins).
  • To sync your data across devices (if you opt-in to cloud sync).
  • To display advertisements (unless you purchase the ad-free version).

3. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services that collect information used to identify you:

  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements.
  • Firebase: For analytics and optional cloud synchronization.
  • Apple Maps: For providing map functionality.

4. Data Storage and Security

By default, your pins, photos, and location data are stored locally on your device. If you choose to use cloud features, your data is securely stored using Firebase services. We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data.

5. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data stored in the app.
  • Delete your account and associated data at any time through the app settings.
  • Opt-out of personalized advertising.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
